Your Safety,Our Priority

Your safety is our top priority. Whether you’re a passenger or a captain, SheDrives ensures every journey is secure, comfortable, and reliable.

Before the Ride

  • Book only through the SheDrives app.
  • Check captain’s name, photo, and vehicle plate before boarding.
  • Wait in a safe, public area.
  • Share your ride details with family or friends.

During the Ride

  • Wear a seatbelt or helmet at all times.
  • Keep valuables secure and out of sight.
  • Monitor your route in the app.
  • Stay alert and use the in-app safety button if needed.

After the Ride

  • Take all belongings before exiting.
  • Confirm trip end in the app and leave a review.
  • Report any concerns to SheDrives Support.

SheDrives Promise: Together, we make every journey safe, respectful, and comfortable.

Before the Ride

  • Keep your vehicle clean, fueled, and in good condition.
  • Carry valid CNIC, license, and vehicle documents.
  • Verify passenger name and pickup details in the app.
  • Stay in safe, visible pickup spots.

During the Ride

  • Greet politely and confirm passenger’s name.
  • Follow traffic laws and speed limits.
  • Keep route visible in navigation for transparency.
  • Avoid unnecessary personal questions.

After the Ride

  • Drop off only at safe, well-lit locations.
  • End trip in the app and thank the passenger.
  • Check for forgotten items.
  • Report incidents or lost items to SheDrives Support.

Courier Guidelines

  • Ensure parcels are securely packed and labeled.
  • Verify delivery address before starting the trip.
  • Follow traffic and safety regulations at all times.
  • Keep the parcel safe from damage or theft.

Sender Guidelines

  • Use proper packaging to prevent damage.
  • Include recipient details clearly on the parcel.
  • Hand over the parcel only to verified couriers from SheDrives.
  • Avoid sending prohibited or hazardous items.

Receiver Guidelines

  • Receive parcels at a safe, visible location.
  • Inspect the package before accepting it.
  • Report any tampering or damage immediately.
  • Confirm receipt in the SheDrives app.
