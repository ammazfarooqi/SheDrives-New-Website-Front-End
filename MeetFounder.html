Meet the Founder

Ammaz Farooqi

Founder of SheDrives

Ammaz Farooqi is the visionary force behind SheDrives Pakistan’s first Ride Hailing platform built exclusively to empower women and the transgender community. With a mission to ensure safe, respectful, and inclusive transport for all, Ammaz laid the foundation of a movement, not just a service.

Driven by empathy and innovation, Ammaz leads with a passion to break social barriers and inspire change through technology. His leadership continues to shape a future where mobility means freedom, opportunity, and safety for everyone.

Our Vision

To revolutionize Ride Hailing in Pakistan by making it inclusive, secure, and empowering one ride at a time.
