Policy & Rules

At SheDrives, safety, respect, and professionalism are at the heart of every ride. To ensure a comfortable experience for everyone, all Captains and Passengers must follow these guidelines:

Our Commitment

At SheDrives, we strive to create a respectful, safe, and professional environment for all. These rules help us ensure the best experience for drivers and riders alike.

SheDrives Policy & Rules

  • Female-Only Platform SheDrives is exclusively for women only both drivers and riders. No male passengers, drivers, or companions are allowed during any ride.
  • Respect & Behavior Treat each other with respect, dignity, and kindness. No harassment, abuse, or inappropriate language will be tolerated. Maintain personal boundaries and professional conduct throughout the ride.
  • Time & Punctuality Captains should reach the pickup location on time. Passengers should be ready when the driver arrives to avoid delays.
  • Vehicle & Personal Cleanliness Captains must keep their vehicle clean, well-maintained, and safe. Passengers are expected to respect the vehicle no damage, littering, or smoking.
  • App Usage & Safety All rides must be booked and completed through the SheDrives app only. No off-app requests are allowed for the safety of both parties. Use the SOS button in the app or contact support if any issue arises during the ride.
  • Required Documents (For Captains) A valid driving license, CNIC, and vehicle documents are mandatory. SheDrives may conduct background checks and training sessions to ensure high service standards.
  • Ratings & Feedback After each ride, both Captain and Passenger should leave a rating and review to help improve the experience for others.
