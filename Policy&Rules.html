Our Commitment

At SheDrives, we strive to create a respectful, safe, and professional environment for all. These rules help us ensure the best experience for drivers and riders alike.

SheDrives Policy & Rules

  • Respect all community members and their privacy.
  • Follow traffic rules strictly while using SheDrives services.
  • Avoid sharing personal or sensitive information on the platform.
  • Any kind of harassment or misbehavior will not be tolerated.
  • Use clean and respectful language in all communication.
  • We reserve the right to block any user violating policies.
