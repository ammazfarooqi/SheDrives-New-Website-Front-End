We are driving change in
communities by empowering people

Our mission is to empower women through driving education, mobility access, and community outreach by 2030.

OUR VISION

To create a Pakistan where:

  • • Women feel safe and confident to travel independently
  • • Every woman has the freedom to earn with dignity
  • • Roads are inclusive and led by female driven mobility
  • • Communities are uplifted through equal opportunities
  • • Empowerment starts with access, safety, and respect

SheDrives is not just transport it's a movement for change.

OUR MISSION

SheDrives is committed to:

  • • Providing safe, female only transport for women and girls
  • • Creating income opportunities for women through driving
  • • Offering free training, licensing & vehicle support
  • • Building a supportive community for female drivers
  • • Promoting gender equality through inclusive mobility
  • • Expanding access to secure and respectful public spaces
  • • Partnering with brands and institutions to scale impact

Through innovation and inclusion, SheDrives is reshaping mobility in Pakistan.
