About Us

Driving Change One Woman at a Time

SheDrives is Pakistan’s first ride hailing service exclusively for women, by women. Our mission is simple yet powerful: to create a safe, reliable, and empowering transport solution where women can ride and drive with confidence. We are not just a mobility platform we are a movement. A movement that enables women to reclaim public spaces, earn with dignity, and feel secure on every road they take.

How Protective is SheDrives?

SheDrives is built with security and comfort at its core exclusively for females and transgender individuals in Pakistan. Here's how it ensures a safe and trusted experience:

Safety Values

We prioritize user safety and ensure our platform remains secure and reliable for everyone.

Social Impact

Our mission is to create a positive impact in communities through education and inclusion.

Empowerment

We empower users by giving them tools and knowledge to grow and contribute meaningfully.

How it Works:

SheDrives ensures your safety with just one tap.

  • Instantly share your live location
  • Notify emergency contacts
  • Alert SheDrives’s support team for immediate help

Your ride, your safety always in control with SheDrives!

Making Transportation Accessible for Those Who Need It Most
